Rails Girls Summer of Code

Core organizer


Rails Girls Summer of Code is a 3 months global scholarship that sponsors women to contribute full-time to Open Source Projects with the help of coaches and mentors. Since 2013, RGSoC has trained 145 students, raised almost 450.000 USD and created a community of over 800 students, coaches, mentors, supervisors and organizers.

On this program I was one of the 4 core organizers. My main areas of responsibility were Marketing, PR, Student's Support and Sponsorship management.
