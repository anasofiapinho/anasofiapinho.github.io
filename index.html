ABOUT

Olá and welcome! My name is Ana Sofia and I am a Digital Marketeer, Multimedia Designer and Project Manager based in Portugal.

I started as an Industrial Engineer in 2007, focused on Lean Manufacturing even before there were such concepts as "Lean Startup". Two years after, my love for startups lead me to London School of Economics for a course in Entrepreneurship and Global Leadership and I've been connected to the tech world ever since.

Being part of incredible projects, from newly founded startups to organizing startup events all over the country, have pushed me to develop skills in project management, marketing and design. You can find more about my work on this section.

Throughout this journey one of the things that makes me most proud is to be part of several organizations to promote diversity in tech like Rails Girls, Geekettes and more recently, Rails Girls Summer of Code. We are building the society of the future, so we must make sure tech is inclusive!

DIVERSITY

I remember being at a coffee shop in Lisbon with a friend when he mentioned Rails Girls for the first time. Long story short, I knew I had to bring those workshops to Portugal and in March of 2013, the first Rails Girls workshop ever organanized in this country happened in Porto.

There are no words to describe the happiness and excitement when we gather women in events around science and technology. I feel privileged to be able to welcome women and other minorities to tech, a place that has always been theirs, but for many many reasons it might seem like they don't belong there.

The United Nations estimates that we need 170 years to reach "gender parity". We really can't wait that long because just like Neil deGrasse Tyson says, "Science and Tecnology are the engines of the economy of the future", and last time I checked, technology is evolving exponentially, so we need to get going if we want to build a future society both diverse and inclusive.

Besides all of my work with other organizations, I am available for writing articles and giving talks about this topic. For speaking enquiries or any other questions, please send an email to me@anasofiapinho.com.
